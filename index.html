    Gesture: The phone is shaken
    Sound: Thunder (synthesized using Faust)

    Design Motivation:
    This mapping connects the physical action of shaking the phone with the powerful, impactful sound of thunder. The gesture of shaking creates an immediate and visceral connection to the sudden, dramatic nature of a thunderclap. When you shake your device, the motion sensors detect the movement and trigger the thunder sound, creating an intuitive mapping where the intensity of the physical action corresponds to the dramatic auditory result.

    This interaction design is motivated by the natural association between sudden movements and powerful sounds. Just as thunder occurs with sudden atmospheric changes, shaking the device creates a sudden motion that naturally pairs with the thunder sound. The use case scenario could be a weather application, a game, or an interactive sound installation where users can "summon" thunder through physical gesture.
