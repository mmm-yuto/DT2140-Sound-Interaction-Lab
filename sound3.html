    • Interaction 3: Shake → Marimba

    Gesture: Shake the phone
    Sound: Marimba (synthesized using Faust)

    How it works:
    • When you shake the device, the marimba sound is triggered
    • Each shake produces a different pitch (randomized between C4 and C5)
    • The sound duration is 100ms per trigger

    Design Motivation:
    This mapping connects the shaking motion of the device with the sound of a marimba. When you shake the device, the marimba sound is triggered, creating a percussive and musical interaction. Each shake produces a different pitch, adding variety to the sound output.

    This interaction design is motivated by the physical analogy between playing a marimba and shaking the device. The use case scenario could be a virtual marimba app, a music education tool, or an interactive percussion instrument where users can play marimba sounds through natural shaking gestures, making music through physical movement.
