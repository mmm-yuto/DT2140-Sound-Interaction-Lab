    Gesture: The phone is used to point straight up
    Sound: Bells (synthesized using Faust)

    Design Motivation:
    This mapping connects the gesture of pointing the device straight up with the sound of bells. The action of raising the device upward mimics the physical motion of pulling a bell rope upward, creating a natural and intuitive connection between gesture and sound. When the device's pitch angle (rotationY) reaches approximately 90 degrees (pointing straight up), the bell sound is triggered.

    This interaction design is motivated by the cultural and physical association between upward movements and bell ringing. In many cultures, bells are rung by pulling a rope upward, and this gesture mapping recreates that physical relationship in a digital context. The use case scenario could be a meditation app, a church bell simulator, or an interactive musical instrument where users can "ring" bells by pointing their device skyward.
